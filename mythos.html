MYTHOS (!)

There is a cave, somewhere people don't go.
Hidden beneath mountains, and behind rocks, it's hard to find. But one day, someone did find this cave. Through luck or magic, the cave revealed itself and nervously the traveler entered.

It was dark and eery at first. But over time the traveler made it her own, transforming the cold cave into a warm home glowing by fire at night. And then one day, as she sat beside the fire place, staring out at the forest teeming with life, she noticed a jar and in that jar was this weird purple liquid bubbling and splashing around.

Carefully, she approached the jar, opening the lid and staring at the liquid. Then she took a sip of the liquid. It didn't taste like much. Yet she quickly felt different. Her fear of what lurked beyond the cave dissapeared. She had always wanted to climb the mountain that towered above the cave but had been nervous to do so... well the next day she made the climb. Her fears and worries were replaced by a burning desire to fufill her dreams.

Over the years more wanderers, lost on their various journeys, stumbled upon the cave. It grew into a community. And in this cave, by the warm embers of the fireplace and fueled by the magical elixir, the travelers achieved their wildest dreams.
