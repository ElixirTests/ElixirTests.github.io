WELCOME TO THE CAVE (!)

Elixir is a NFT project consisting of 10,000 unique female characters. Each character contains a multitude of intricate, carefully designed traits.

We all have ambitions that we're striving to achieve and ideas that we can't stop thinking about. The passion and desire that motivate us to achieve our goals... well we call that elixir.

So join us, enter the cave and meet your new friends. Together we'll give each other a boost and encourage our fellow creators, collectively chasing the elixir that enables us to turn our dreams into reality.

THE CHASE

We want to build a community of people who chase that special something we call elixir. People who want to build together, learn together, grow together, and just have fun together.

Chasing the elixir is about striving for your goals, achieiving your ambitions, bettering yourself and we belive that there's no better way to chase the elixir than with each other.

Your backgrounds, experiences, perspectives and ideas are what make you unique, and what gives our community value.

So, wherever you are in the world and wherever you are in life, we'd love for you to join us, enter the cave, and embark on this special quest together. Let's all chase the elixir!
