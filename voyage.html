OUR VOYAGE (!)

Our voyage is our roadmap. The journey we plan to embark on with you all. We are an art focused project mainly driven by our community. So our roadmap is a set of loose ideas. Possible future projects that we are flexible enough to alter if you all desire.

Too often projects overpromise and wildly underdeliver. We won't make that same mistake.

So check out our values and see what we're planning for the future. We're really excited. Are you?

our values

art focused
The Elixir is an art focused NFT project. Too often projects neglect what we see as the most fundamental aspect of any character based NFT project: the art. With Elixir, we haven't made that same mistake. Art has been our foremost priority and we've spent many months ensuring that every detail is perfect. We can't wait for you to see your character and we are confident you will fall in love with the illustration.

unique and valuable
With so many NFT projects out there (many of which are lazy derivatives of others) we want our project to be unique. This means unique art and a unique roadmap instead of the generic cookie cutter steps that so many NFT projects stick to. We also don’t like when projects stuff their roadmap with easy to complete tasks just to say they have a roadmap and they’ve ticked off some of the boxes. If we pursue something we want it to be of value to you all and we promise that our roadmap will only consists of ideas that we truly believe are worthwhile to pursue.

you decide
We are brimming with ideas and so excited to set out on this voyage with you all. But we know that together you all can help guide this journey and we value your insights. Ultiamtely, we want you all to determine our next steps suggesting ideas you’d like to see us pursue and giving us feedback on our current plans. And so, we want our roadmap to be viewed as a set of loose ideas. Possible future projects that we are flexible enough to alter if you all desire.

community

support community artists
We know all lot of you are incredibly talented artists and we want to help support and highlight your work. Every month we will have a competition where the best art pieces from the community will be highlighted on our social medias. We view this as a great opportunity to use our platform and reach to help other talented creators show off their work.

contribute to elixir mythos
The mythos of elixir is still shrouded in mystery. We’ve unveiled the first drops but we want you all to help us unravel the rest. We will host monthly story writing contests for elixir holders where anyone inclined can help write up the next stage of Elixir's mythos. The community will then decide which contribution they would like to see codified as the next part of Elixir’s mythos and it will subsequently be added to the mythos section on our website (of course acknowledging the winner as the contributor).

community votes
These competitions only work if they allow community members to vote. So we will be using the elixir website to host these competitions where community members can connect with their wallet containing an Elixir NFT and easily vote in the monthly competitions. We believe this will further foster community and encourage our creators to share their work with like-minded peers.

building

gallery
After Launch, we will build out a gallery to display all Elixir NFTs. The gallery will enable easy filtering of characters by traits and features and will help highlight the wonderful artwork and allow you all to show off your characters.

monthly art drops
We will release custom illustrations of new characters, environments or anything else we think would look awesome to all Elixir holders each month. We may work to ensure these illustrations can be acquired as NFTs if desired.

tipping mechanism
As we have emphasized, we really value you all. And so we want you to be rewarded for your talent, skills and ideas. In addition to hosting these monthly art, and story writing contests which add to the vibrancy off the elixir community and narrative we also will explore creating some mechanism for the participants in these competitions to receive tips from fellow community members who value their contributions. This would likely look like a separate page on the elixir website featuring the past winners and other participants and some built in easy to use mechanism that allows everyone to tip their favorite creators.

the future

second collection
Maybe…. we’re not sure of course. This would be a big endeavor and would depend on you all and the support we receive but we may explore building a 2.0 collection. The characters that make up the 2.0 collection would be unique in some aspect (Male and female? Unique environments?) where existing Elixir holders get 1 NFT for free. We would build this 2.0 collection in collaboration with you all, shaping the project based on the ideas and feedback you share.

up to you
As you can tell, however, the distant future for Elixir is still up in the air. We want to do what you as a community value and so we hope that you all can help guide our future. We are excited to hear your voices which we are confident will push us in the perfect direction long term.

Together we will chase the elixir—now and forever.
